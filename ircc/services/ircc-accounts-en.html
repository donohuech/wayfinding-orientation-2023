There are different accounts for various immigration and citizenship applications, paying, checking status and making changes. Before you register or sign in, make sure you're using the right account.

IRCC accounts

  • IRCC Secure Account to apply to immigrate, for Express Entry, for International Experience Canada, and for work and study permits
  • Apply for a visitor visa - instructions for your situation
  • Transit Visa to apply for a transit visa to travel through Canada in 48 hours or less
  • Permanent residents: apply for Canadian citizenship online
